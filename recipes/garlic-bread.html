GARLIC BREAD

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4-6 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup parsley, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup Parmigiano Reggiano, grated
  • 1 large ciabatta loaf

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, add unsalted butter, chopped garlic, finely chopped parsley, and grated Parmigiano Reggiano.
    Mix together until thoroughly combined.
  2. Cut a large loaf of ciabatta bread in half lengthwise.
  3. Using a flat end spatula, spread the garlic butter across the bread. Coat edge to edge.
  4. Place the bread on a foil-lined rimmed baking sheet and place in a preheated oven set to 400F for 10-12 minutes.
